# Elective E06 — AI Around the World: Trends & Policy (Simple)

*Short elective · 60 minutes · All members · Leadership encouraged*

**Slides:** [slides/E06_ai_world_trends.md](slides/E06_ai_world_trends.md)

---

## Objectives

1. Place **national S&T platform** work in **global context**  
2. Recognize **regulation themes** (privacy, transparency, risk tiers)  
3. Avoid hype — **AGI** vs today’s tools ([02_concept_agi.md](../02_concept_agi.md))  

---

## 1. Global adoption landscape (12 min)

| Trend | Plain description |
|-------|-------------------|
| **Enterprise assistants** | Org-specific Q&A over documents |
| **Open-weight models** | Run locally ([05_local_llm.md](../05_local_llm.md)) |
| **Regulation** | EU AI Act themes, national AI strategies |
| **Public sector pilots** | Citizen bots, internal copilots |
| **Multimodal** | Text + image + audio interfaces |

**Not one winner** — mix of cloud, local, hybrid.

---

## 2. Regulatory themes (not legal advice) (15 min)

| Theme | Org response |
|-------|--------------|
| **Risk-based approach** | Our T0–T3 tiers ([08](08_trust_verification.md)) |
| **Transparency** | Citations, disclaimers |
| **Data protection** | Classification ([09](09_privacy_security.md)) |
| **Human oversight** | T2/T3 gates |
| **Documentation** | Model cards, corpus register |

**Facilitator:** Cite your jurisdiction’s official guidance — do not invent law in slides.

---

## 3. AGI hype vs today (10 min)

| Today | AGI (not achieved) |
|-------|---------------------|
| LLM + RAG + tools | General reliable autonomy |
| Narrow + probabilistic | Broad human-like judgment |

See [02_concept_agi.md](../02_concept_agi.md).

---

## 4. National S&T / innovation context (10 min)

Discussion frames (customize for your country):

- **Sovereignty** — local models for sensitive research data  
- **Competitiveness** — faster admin for grants/patents  
- **Trust** — citizen confidence in public AI  
- **Talent** — literacy programs (this curriculum)  

---

## 5. Activity — headline triage (12 min)

Give groups 5 news headlines (prepare cards):

| Headline type | Response |
|---------------|----------|
| “AGI achieved” | Skeptical — check source |
| “Ban all AI” | Overbroad — risk-based |
| “Local LLM for government” | Aligns with [05](05_local_llm.md) |
| “Chatbot wrong deadline” | Process + corpus issue |

---

## 6. Takeaways

1. **Global move** toward governed assistants, not only consumer chat.  
2. **Regulation** aligns with tiers, transparency, humans.  
3. **AGI headlines** ≠ today’s deployment tasks.  
4. **This curriculum** is your org’s literacy foundation.
