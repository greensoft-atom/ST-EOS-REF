# Elective E03 — AI in Research, Innovation & Public Sector

*Short elective · 60 minutes · All members*  
*General use cases — not org product roadmap*

**Slides:** [slides/E03_research_public_sector.md](slides/E03_research_public_sector.md)

---

## Objectives

1. Map **use cases** across research, patents, funding, policy, exhibitions  
2. Assign **risk tier** preview (T0–T3) ([08_trust_verification.md](../08_trust_verification.md))  
3. See **stacked workflow** recognition → RAG → generative → human  

---

## 1. Sector map (10 min)

```
Research ── literature, lab notes, collaboration
Innovation ── patents, startups, tech transfer
Funding ── calls, eligibility, reporting
Policy ── briefings, citizen info
Exhibitions ── content, visitor FAQ
```

---

## 2. Use case table (20 min)

| Sector | Recognition AI | Generative + RAG | Human must |
|--------|----------------|------------------|------------|
| **Research** | Paper classification | Literature summary + cite | Verify DOIs |
| **Patents** | Prior art search assist | Draft background | Attorney files |
| **Funding** | Form completeness check | FAQ from circulars | Award decision |
| **Policy** | Doc routing | Plain-language draft | Legal approve |
| **Exhibitions** | Image tagging | Caption draft | Comms approve |

---

## 3. Stacked workflow (10 min)

```
Ingest archives → classify (R)
       → index for RAG
       → answer / summarize (G)
       → VERIFY → approve → publish
```

From [03_gai_rai.md](../03_gai_rai.md) + [06_rag.md](../06_rag.md).

---

## 4. Activity — tier the use case (15 min)

| Scenario | Tier | Why |
|----------|------|-----|
| Auto-tag 50k papers by field | T1 | Human samples quality |
| Public grant FAQ with cites | T2 | Approve before web |
| AI decides grant winner | **No** | T3 human only |
| Brainstorm session titles | T0 | Low risk |

## 6. Takeaways

1. Same AI stack, different **stakes** per sector.  
2. **Recognition** still does bulk indexing.  
3. **Generative** assists language; **humans** decide high stakes.
